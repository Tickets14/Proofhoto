# Proofhoto App Overview

## What is this app?

Proofhoto is an offline-first habit tracker where habits are only marked complete after the user submits proof.

Proof can be:
- A photo
- A short video (up to 15 seconds)

This makes habit tracking more honest and measurable by linking each completion to real visual evidence.

## Key features

- Proof-based completion using camera or gallery media
- Habit management: create, edit, reorder, and archive habits
- Weekly scheduling by weekday with optional reminder times
- Video proof support with generated thumbnail previews
- Timeline view grouped by day for browsing past completions
- Stats dashboard with streak tracking and habit trends
- 7-day completion chart for short-term performance
- Monthly heatmap for long-term consistency tracking
- Streak freeze system to protect streaks when a day is missed
- Theme options: system, light, and dark mode
- Local JSON export for backup and portability
- 100% local data storage (Hive + app documents directory)

## Privacy and data

- No cloud account is required
- Habit data and proof media are stored locally on the device
- Users keep direct control over their own records and exports
